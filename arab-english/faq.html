  • Can I participate if I am not a resident in an Arab country, knowing that I have an Arab nationality?

    UNFORTUNATELY, REGISTRATION FOR THE TOURNAMENT IS ONLY RESTRICTED TO PLAYERS RESIDING (I.E. LIVING) IN ONE OF THE PARTICIPATING COUNTRIES.

  • Do all team members have to be of the same nationality?

    IF THEY RESIDE IN THE SAME COUNTRY, THERE IS NOTHING WRONG WITH PLAYERS BELONGING TO DIFFERENT NATIONALITIES. FOR EXAMPLE: PLAYERS RESIDING IN SAUDI ARABIA AND HAVE DIFFERENT NATIONALITIES CAN FORM A TEAM TO COMPETE IN THE LOCAL TOURNAMENT.

  • Are there any financial costs to participate in the tournament?

    PARTICIPATION IN THE TOURNAMENT IS TOTALLY FREE FOR ALL CITIZENS AND RESIDENTS IN THE PARTICIPATING COUNTRIES.

  • Can I participate in the tournament if I do not belong to an Arab nationality?

    YES, YOU CAN JOIN THE TOURNAMENT PROVIDED THAT YOU ARE A RESIDENT OF ONE OF THE PARTICIPATING COUNTRIES, AS YOU CAN ONLY JOIN THE LOCAL TOURNAMENT IN THE COUNTRY IN WHICH YOU CURRENTLY LIVE.

  • Is there a minimum level of players to be eligible to participate in the tournament?

    NO, ANY PLAYER OF ANY LEVEL CAN PARTICIPATE IN THE TOURNAMENT, BUT HE MUST HAVE AT LEAST 20 CHAMPIONS IN HIS ACCOUNT.

  • Can I change my team information after the registration period ends?

    YOU CANNOT MAKE ANY CHANGES TO YOUR TEAM INFORMATION AFTER THE REGISTRATION PERIOD ENDS, AND ANY ATTEMPT TO DO SO WILL RESULT DISQUALIFYING YOUR TEAM FROM THE TOURNAMENT.

  • What is the permitted number of reserve players in each team?

    EACH TEAM IS ALLOWED TO HAVE TWO RESERVE PLAYERS.

  • Can we replace a team player in an emergency?

    THE TEAM IS ALLOWED TO ASK ONE OF THE RESERVE PLAYERS TO JOIN THE GAME IN PLACE OF THE MAIN PLAYER IN CASE OF AN EMERGENCY.

  • Can I join another team if my team is out of competition?

    IN ORDER TO PRESERVE THE INTEGRITY OF THE COMPETITION, ITS NOT ALLOWED FOR A PLAYER FROM A LOSING TEAM TO JOIN ANY OTHER TEAM DURING THE TOURNAMENT, AND HE WILL BE CONSIDERED COMPLETELY OUT OF COMPETITION.

  • How many matches are there in each stage of the tournament?

    THE NUMBER OF MATCHES DEPENDS ON THE NUMBER OF TEAMS PARTICIPATING IN EACH TOURNAMENT.

  • Where are the tournament activities held?

    THE MATCHES WILL BE HELD AND ORGANIZED VIA ARAB ESPORTS PLATFORM HTTPS://WWW.ARABESPORTS.COM

  • What are the procedures to be followed in case my team or one of the team members encounters an internet connection problem?

    EACH TEAM WILL RECEIVE A 15-MINUTE BREAK FOR EVERY MATCH PLAYED IN THE TOURNAMENT, IN ORDER TO DEAL WITH ANY EMERGENCY ISSUES AFFECTING THE NETWORK CONNECTION. PLAYERS ARE ADVISED TO CHECK THEIR INTERNET CONNECTION ON THE DAY OF THE MATCH AND TAKE NECESSARY MEASURES TO AVOID SUCH PROBLEMS IN ALL CASES.

  • How do I know when the date of my team match in the tournament?

    YOU CAN FOLLOW ALL THE MATCH INFORMATION (DATE - TIME - THE OPPONENT) THROUGH THE OFFICIAL ARAB ESPORTS PLATFORM.

  • How do I know which side my team will play in ?

    THE TEAMS THAT WILL PLAY ON THE BLUE SIDE OR THE RED SIDE IN THE FIELD WILL BE DETERMINED BASED ON THEIR POSITIONS IN THE MATCH SCHEDULE, WHICH IS AVAILABLE ON THE OFFICIAL ARAB ESPORTS PLATFORM.

  • How can we enter the match room of our team?

    A NOTIFICATION OF THE ROOM CODE WILL BE SENT TO THE ARAB ESPORTS PLATFORM AT THE START OF THE MATCH, SO WE KINDLY ASK YOU TO FOLLOW UP ON THE SITE CONTINUOUSLY TO LEARN ABOUT THE TOURNAMENT'S NEWS.

  • What do I do if I find a violation of the rules during the match?

    IN THE CASE THAT THE RULES OF THE TOURNAMENT ARE NOT RESPECTED, PLEASE CONTACT DIRECTLY WITH ONE OF THE ORGANIZERS VIA DISCORD, WITH CLEAR EVIDENCE OF THE VIOLATION (SCREENSHOT OF THE VIOLATION).

  • What is the penalty for breaking the rules?

    WHEN A PARTICIPANT VIOLATES THE SPECIFIED RULES, THE ENTIRE TEAM IS DISQUALIFIED FROM THE TOURNAMENT, AND HE MAY BE PREVENTED FROM PARTICIPATING IN THE UPCOMING ARAB ESPORTS TOURNAMENTS IN THE FUTURE.

  • What's the cashprize offered in the tournament?

    PRIZES WILL BE ALLOCATED - DIVIDED INTO THE FIRST THREE TEAMS AS FOLLOWS: - FIRST PLACE 2700 AED - SECOND PLACE 1750 AED - THIRD PLACE 750 AED.

  • How can I receive the prize if I win?

    PRIZES WILL BE AWARDED WITHIN 30 DAYS OF THE TOURNAMENT DATE.
